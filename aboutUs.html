Q&A (Questions and Answers)

What is Times Art?
Times Art is a digital art showcase museum, where artists from across the world can send in their own art via email, to be digitally displayed in the art gallery, and may even be displayed in the "Top 30 Weekly Art Showcase".
How was Times Art created?
Times Art was a website idea made by the creator "Tobi Thai", who's idea was to display his and potentialy anyone elses art, to be digitally admired and viewed by others.
How does the "Top 30 Weekly Art Showcase" work?
The idea works via the number of likes given from the art in the gallery tab. Calculating the likes, the top 30 most liked art pieces will be displayed for next weeks Top 30 Art Showcase.

If you have any questions regarding sending art or in need of more info about the website, you may visit the Contact Us page.
