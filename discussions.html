Discussions

How do I blend with markers?

- You can blend markers by swiping them to make layers. The more swipes you do, the more darker the color.

What's that thing around the sun, and how do you use it?

- That type of art style is called "hatching" and "cross-hatching." It's a technique used to fill in empty spaces, or to make things look dark, without wasting tons of black art material.

How do I start drawing? I'm nervous it will look bad.

- Just start drawing. Your first drawing may not look the best, but consistency and learning it what will make you improve on drawing. Just start now, and look up some things on Youtube if you want to learn that specific thing.

How do I draw digitally?

I use to be a traditional artist, but when I picked up digital drawing, it doesn't look as good as my traditional drawings. What do I do?

- You would just have to keep practicing and learning how it works. A bike and a e-bike are the same of two different things. Keep practicing, you got this!
